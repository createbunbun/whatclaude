Usage Tips

How to use Claude

Practical example prompts that even beginners can use right away
Organized by category.

🏠 Use in daily life

Make your daily life more convenient. Feel free to talk to Claude

communication

✉️ Compose emails/letters

From business emails to private messages, it will suggest texts that suit the occasion.

example prompt
Please write an apology email to your business partner. We apologize for the 3 day delay in delivery.
Thinking of wedding congratulatory messages to your friends. It feels casual and warm.
Write a note apologizing for missing the PTA meeting.
Going out

✈️ Travel planning

Just tell us your destination, dates, and budget, and we will suggest a travel plan that suits you.

example prompt
Make a Kyoto trip plan for 3 nights and 4 days. I want to enjoy visiting temples and shrines and eating gourmet food. The budget is 50,000 yen per person.
We made a list of things to pack for a trip to Hawaii as a family of four (kids are 5 and 8 years old).
Please tell me 5 recommended spots that you can go on a day trip to during next month's 3-day weekend (from Tokyo).
meal

🍳 Cooking/Recipes

It helps you come up with recipes from leftovers in the fridge and create nutritionally balanced menus.

example prompt
Please tell me 3 easy recipes that you can make using the chicken and cabbage you have in the fridge. Cook in less than 20 minutes.
Think of a week's worth of dinner menus. The budget for a family of four is 10,000 yen, and we would like to focus on Japanese food.
My child hates vegetables. Please tell me some delicious recipes to eat vegetables.
shopping

🛒 Shopping consultation

If you're having trouble choosing a product, ask Claude. It will help you organize comparison points.

example prompt
Compare three recommended robot vacuum cleaners within 50,000 yen. For households with pets.
Please tell me which mirrorless single-lens camera is recommended for beginners. Budget around 150,000 yen.
I want my elementary school children to learn programming. Compare recommended teaching materials and services.

💼 Use at work

✨ Tips for effective use

With a little effort, Claude's answers can be much better.

1. Give specific instructions

Rather than asking vague questions, you will get a more accurate answer if you specifically explain the conditions and background.

❌ Ambiguous example

"Tell me which computer you recommend"

✅ Specific examples

"With a budget of 100,000 yen, what are three recommended laptops for writing university reports and watching videos? Focus on lightness."

❌ Ambiguous example

"Write me an email"

✅ Specific examples

``Write an email to your business partner, Company A, to arrange a meeting schedule for next week.Suggested dates are Tuesday and Thursday afternoons.Speak in a polite tone.''

2. Give a role

You can give Claude an expert role and get answers specific to that field.

You are a professional editor. Please proofread the text below to make it easier to read.
You are a web designer with 10 years of experience. Please teach beginners the basics of site color scheme.
You are a registered dietitian. Please suggest a daily meal plan for those on a diet.

3. Ask questions step by step

Rather than asking questions about complex topics all at once, you can get deeper answers by asking questions in stages.

💡
Step 1:“Teach me the basic framework of marketing.”
Step 2:“Please tell me more about the 4P analysis sometime soon.”
Step 3:"Thinking about my cafe's strategy using 4P analysis"

By asking the next question based on the previous answer, you can deepen the conversation and get more practical advice.

4. Specify the output format

If you tell them the format you want first, they will respond in a format that is easy to use.

Tabular format bullet points number list markdown JSON
The following information is summarized in a table format with 3 columns of "Item/Contents/Notes".
List 5 advantages and 5 disadvantages in bullet points.
Summarize the steps in a numbered list, with each step no more than two lines long.

5. Utilize rework/additional instructions

It's okay if your first answer isn't perfect. Can be adjusted with additional instructions.

Rewrite it in a more casual tone.
Keep it short, no more than 300 characters.
Good content, but please include concrete numerical data.
