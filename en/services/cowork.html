Cowork

Cowork

Claude will work in the background while you work on other tasks

What is Cowork?

Easy explanation for first-time users

Differences from normal chat

A normal chat is completed with one round trip of "question → answer".
Cowork is different. All you have to do is say, ``Do this,'' and Claude will automatically complete many steps behind the scenes.

As an image,An AI assistant is working silently behind the scenes.It's a feeling. While you work on other tasks or grab a cup of coffee, Claude completes the tasks you ask him to do.

Supported platforms

  • 🖥️ Mac desktop app ○ Compatible
  • 🪟 Windows desktop app ○ Compatible
  • 🌐 Browser version (claude.ai) ✕ Cannot be used
  • 📱 Smartphone app (iOS/Android) ✕ Cannot be used
💡
Requires desktop app installation. The Cowork function is not available in the browser version or smartphone app. If you do not have it yet, please download it by referring to "How to get started" below.

How to get started (3 steps)

1

Download the desktop app

claude.ai/download Go to and download the app for your OS (Mac or Windows).

2

Open the app and log in

Launch the downloaded app and log in with your Claude account. Make sure you're subscribed to a Pro plan or higher.

3

Select and instruct "Cowork" mode

Select "Cowork" mode on the chat screen and instruct the work you want done. Claude will do the rest automatically in the background.
